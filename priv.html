This includes Information relating to your use of our mobile games (“Mobile Games”, “Games”). When you use the Mobile Games, you accept our rules and policies which set out how we handle your Information, and you understand that we collect, process, use and store your Information as described in this Policy. Information we automatically collect or is generated about you when you play the Games Identifiers, such as your IP address, device and app ID, unique user ID, city level location data and device information (such as model, brand and operating system). You have the option to permit installation of such Cookies or subsequently disable them. You may accept all cookies, or instruct the device or web browser to provide notice at the time of installation of cookies, or refuse to accept all cookies by adjusting the relevant cookie retention function in your device. Information you generate and display when playing the Games, such as profile photos and chat content. Information received from third parties Information we receive from third party platforms when you register: when you register through a third party account (Apple, Facebook or Google Play), we may receive your third party ID, name, avatar and city level location. Information from payment processors: when you make payments in our Games, we receive information from our payment processors about your transactions, including your Apple ID number for Apple, your postcode and state for Google. Information collected from advertising and analytics partners: we receive information from Unity Technologies (a US and Finnish company). This includes user ID and information on your interactions with advertising shown in on our Mobile Games. Unity Technologies provides reports that help us optimize our in-game features and events to each user of our Mobile Games which include your user ID and information on your in-game activity. Information from mobile measurement partners: we receive information from third parties to allow us to track performance of our marketing campaigns and to detect fraud in such campaigns. This includes advertising ID, IP address, location and transaction information. Children Our Games may be targeted for children.

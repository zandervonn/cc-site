How to Play

  • PLAYING: Swipe on the screen to launch your planets.
  • MERGE: When two planets of the same type collide, they combine into the next largest. Work your way up to the sun.
  • ORDER: In order of scale - Pluto -> Mercury -> Mars -> Venus -> Earth -> Neptune -> Uranus -> Saturn -> Jupiter -> The Sun
